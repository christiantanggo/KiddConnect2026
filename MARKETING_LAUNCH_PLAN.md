# Tavari AI Phone Agent - Social Media Marketing Launch Plan

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Small to medium businesses (restaurants, salons, auto shops, medical practices)
- Business owners who miss calls during busy hours
- Companies looking to reduce phone staffing costs
- Businesses wanting 24/7 availability

## 📱 Platform Strategy

### 1. **LinkedIn** (Primary B2B Channel) ⭐ HIGHEST PRIORITY
**Why**: B2B decision-makers are here, excellent for thought leadership

**Quick Start:**
- Create company page: "Tavari AI Phone Agent"
- Personal profile optimization for founder
- Join relevant groups: "Small Business Owners", "Restaurant Technology", "Healthcare Business", etc.

**Content Strategy:**
- **Week 1-2**: Educational posts about phone automation, missed call costs
- **Week 3-4**: Case studies, testimonials, demo videos
- **Ongoing**: Industry insights, tips, behind-the-scenes

**Posting Frequency**: 3-5 times/week
**Best Times**: 8-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM (weekdays)

**Content Ideas:**
- "How much do missed calls cost your business?"
- "3 ways AI phone agents increase revenue"
- Customer success stories
- Video demos showing the AI in action
- Industry statistics and data

---

### 2. **Instagram** (Visual & Testimonial Channel)
**Why**: Great for visual demos, testimonials, building brand personality

**Quick Start:**
- Business account: @tavari.ai (or @tavari.phone or available variation)
- Bio: "AI Phone Agent | Never miss a call | 24/7 availability | Try free demo → [link]"
- Link in bio tool: Linktree with demo signup, pricing, contact

**Content Strategy:**
- **Reels (60-90% of content)**: 
  - Quick demos of AI answering calls
  - Before/after scenarios
  - Customer testimonials
  - "Day in the life" of the AI agent
  - Behind-the-scenes of building the product
  
- **Carousel Posts**: 
  - 5 ways AI saves time
  - Pricing plans comparison
  - Industry use cases
  - Feature highlights

- **Stories**:
  - Daily tips
  - Polls about business pain points
  - Q&A sessions
  - Customer highlights

**Posting Frequency**: Daily (mix of Reels, posts, stories)
**Best Times**: 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM, 8-9 PM

**Hashtags Strategy** (use 20-30 per post):
- Broad: #smallbusiness #entrepreneur #businessowner
- Specific: #aireceptionist #phoneautomation #businessautomation
- Niche: #restauranttech #salontech #autoshopowner
- Branded: #tavari #tavariai

---

### 3. **TikTok** (Viral Potential & Education)
**Why**: Algorithm favors new accounts, perfect for quick educational content

**Quick Start:**
- Business account: @tavari.ai
- Bio: "AI phone agent that never misses calls 📞✨ | Free demo in bio"
- Link in bio: Direct to demo/signup

**Content Strategy:**
- **Educational Series**: "3 things AI can do that receptionists can't"

- Trending audio/sounds with business twist

**Posting Frequency**: 1-2 times/day
**Best Times**: 6-10 AM, 7-9 PM

**Hashtags Strategy** (use 5-10 per video):
- Trending: #smallbusiness #entrepreneur #businesstips
- Specific: #aireceptionist #phoneautomation #businesshacks
- Niche: #restaurantowner #salonowner #autoshop

## 💰 Paid Advertising Strategy (When Ready)

### Priority Order:
1. **LinkedIn Ads** - Target business owners, decision-makers
   - Best for: Direct sales, B2B lead generation
   - Budget: Start with $20-50/day

2. **Instagram/Facebook Ads** - Target small business owners
   - Best for: Brand awareness, demos
   - Budget: Start with $10-30/day

3. **TikTok Ads** - Target younger business owners
   - Best for: Viral reach, brand awareness
   - Budget: Start with $10-20/day

### Ad Creative Ideas:
- Demo video (30-60 seconds)
- Before/after comparison
- Customer testimonial videos
- Problem/solution format

## 📈 Growth Tactics

### 1. **Content Repurposing**
- Turn 1 video into: TikTok, Instagram Reel, YouTube Short, LinkedIn video
- Turn 1 blog post into: LinkedIn article, Twitter thread, Instagram carousel, email newsletter

### 2. **Engagement Strategy**
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours
- Engage with competitor's audience (helpfully, not salesy)
- Join conversations in relevant hashtags
- Share user-generated content

### 3. **Partnership Strategy**
- Partner with business coaches/consultants
- Collaborate with complementary SaaS products
- Guest post on business blogs
- Podcast appearances

### 4. **Influencer Outreach**
- Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) in business space
- Offer free trial in exchange for honest review
- Affiliate program for referrals

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Track these weekly:
- **Follower growth** (by platform)

## 🎨 Brand Voice & Messaging

**Tone**: Helpful, approachable, confident (not salesy)
**Key Messages**:
- "Never miss a call again"
- "24/7 availability without 24/7 costs"
- "AI that actually understands your business"
- "Set up in minutes, not months"

**Avoid**: 
- Overly technical jargon
- Making unrealistic promises
- Negative competitor comparisons
