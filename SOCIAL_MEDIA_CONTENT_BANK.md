## 💡 Pro Tips for Content Creation

1. **Repurpose Everything**: Turn 1 video into TikTok, Reel, YouTube Short, LinkedIn video
2. **Use Real Numbers**: Share actual metrics when possible
3. **Show, Don't Tell**: Demos > descriptions
4. **User-Generated Content**: Share customer stories (with permission)
5. **Trending Audio**: Use trending sounds but make them relevant to business
6. **Consistency**: Post at the same times daily
7. **Engage**: Respond to every comment, especially in first 30 minutes
8. **Test**: Try different formats, see what works, double down
